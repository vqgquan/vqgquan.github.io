The Mark of Athena is the spectacular third installment in the bestselling series Heroes of Olympus by Rick Riordan. One fatal prophecy, seven courageous demigods, and a mission to locate and shut the portals of death. Annabeth felt as if a frigid washcloth had been draped around her neck. She heard the presence's whispered mirth again, as if it had followed her off the ship. She viewed the Argo II from above. Its enormous bronze hull glistened in the sun. A portion of her desired to immediately abduct Percy, board the ship, and flee the area while they still could. She could not escape the feeling that something terrible was about to occur.
