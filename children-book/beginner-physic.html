Physics For Beginners

Author

Publisher
Publisher A

Summary

Part of the best-selling '...for Beginners' series, this book brings the vast realm of physics to life. This book focuses on explaining what various branches of physics are about, how we know about them, and what mysteries remain for aspiring physicists to uncover, rather than providing instruction on how to perform physics. It tackles difficult topics such as black holes, quantum mechanics, and Einstein's theories of relativity.
