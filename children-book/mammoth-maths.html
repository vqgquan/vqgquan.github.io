Mammoth Maths: Everything You Need to Know About Number

Author
DK, David Macaulay
Publisher
DK Children
Publishing date
2022

Summary

David Macaulay, a renowned author and illustrator, has created a unique aid to mathematics.

Math is everywhere...if you look attentively enough! Mathematics is genuinely pervasive, from computer games to bridges to shopping malls and game shows. David Macauley's fantastic ensemble of inquisitive mammoths will guide children ages 8 and up through the fundamentals of mathematics, including numbers, calculation, geometry, and measurement.

In Mammoth Maths, you will not only learn the fundamentals of mathematics, but you will also appreciate doing so! The mammoths are your guide from beginning to end as they endeavor to comprehend mathematics!

These intrepid demonstrators will go to extraordinary measures to educate and entertain as they grapple with adding or subtracting numbers, measuring angles, creating a pie chart, and solving equations, among other mathematical challenges. David Macaulay, a best-selling illustrator, observes and records the mammoths' behavior. Macaulay captures the oddball humor of his subject matter, making Mammoth Maths the ideal introduction to mathematics for young learners. Macaulay is renowned for his ability to explain complex concepts with straightforward genius.

Discover mathematics as you've never known it with:
-Fun-filled illustrations depict Macaulay's mammoths investigating mathematical concepts in unusual and amusing ways, demonstrating fundamental mathematical principles.
- An exciting alternative to dry and unappealing mathematics manuals.
-Supporting panels include diagrams and additional information to facilitate comprehension.
-The book is divided into chapters, each of which focuses on a distinct area of mathematics.
Over 60 topics, including numbers, geometry, measurement, and operations, are covered in Mammoth Maths, making it the ideal maths book for all children aged 8 and up, as well as for reluctant maths learners who don't believe maths is for them. Comprises brand-new illustrations of Macaulay's signature mammoths, which are familiar to readers of The Way Things Work, a best-selling book by David Macaulay that has sold over three million copies worldwide!
Join the math expedition today! The solution is only one mammoth journey away!
