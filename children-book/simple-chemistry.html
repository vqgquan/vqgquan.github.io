Chemistry: The Ultimate Bitesize Study Guide (Supersimple)

Author
DK
Publisher
Dorling Kindersley
Publishing date
2020

Summary

This comprehensive guide to the Next Generation Science Standards for grades 6 to 10 is an excellent aid for coursework, homework, and test preparation, and will have you ready for tests and exams in no time. Each subject is comprehensively illustrated to support the information, clarify the facts, and bring the science to life. A large central image visually explains the concept, and each topic is summarized on a single page, allowing children to rapidly grasp how chemistry works. Information boxes explain the theory with the aid of simple graphics, and a Key Facts box provides a basic summary that can be reviewed for future reference. SuperSimple Chemistry is the ideal accessible guide to chemistry for children, complementing classroom instruction and making exam preparation easier than ever before.
