General Knowledge Genius!: A Quiz Encyclopedia To Boost Your Brain

Author
DK
Publisher
DK Children
Publishing date
2019

Summary

A fantastic examination book for intelligent children; put your general knowledge to the test and astound your family and friends with your intelligence.

Can you identify Europe's longest river? Do you recognize the cranium from the sternum? Are you familiar with archaeopteryx and allosaurus? Can you identify the Indian and Italian flags?

You could!

What are you waiting for then? Open the pages of General Knowledge Genius to determine your level of general knowledge, and then push yourself to learn more!

With over sixty topics spanning the encyclopedia, there is something for everyone. The pages are filled with stunning images, but do you know what they depict? To assist you, "Test Yourself" columns list the information you seek. With three levels of difficulty, the challenge becomes increasingly difficult as you progress from Starter to Challenger to Genius. If you need a hint, there is an interesting fact associated with each image.

Compete in General Knowledge Brilliant mental challenge!

A fantastic examination book for intelligent children; put your general knowledge to the test and astound your family and friends with your intelligence.
