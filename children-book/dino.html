My Book Of Dinosaurs And Prehistoric Life: Animals And Plants To Amaze, Surprise, And Astonish!

Author
DK, Dean R. Lomax
Publisher
Dorling Kindersley Ltd
Publishing date
2021

Summary

Travel back in time to learn about the fantastical animals that inhabited Earth before humans.

From the first living cells to terrifying dinosaurs and gigantic mammals, take a voyage through prehistory to learn about the gigantic, terrifying, and simply bizarre animals and plants that once inhabited Earth. There are profiles on 50 key species, including well-known favorites such as the colossal Tyrannosaurus and the enormous woolly mammoths, as well as lesser-known organisms such as the sail-backed Dimetrodon and the airplane-sized pterosaur Quetzalcoatlus.

Learn about the primordial world and how the Earth has changed over time, the formation of fossils, and the emergence of early humans. Detailed artworks bring the past to life, pronunciation guides assist with difficult names, and a visual index provides a fast overview of the book's most important species. My Book of Dinosaurs and Prehistoric Life is an ideal introduction to early plants and animals and is certain to be a success with fact-hungry young dinosaur and prehistoric life enthusiasts.
