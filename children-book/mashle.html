MASHLE 7

Author
Hajime Komoto
Publisher
Kim Đồng
Publishing date
2023

Summary

In the magic domain, magic is everything, and one's skill with it determines their social standing. Mash dwells in the forest, oblivious to the ways of the world. Due to his daily workouts, he has become a fitness deity. When Mash is discovered, he is forced to enroll in magic school, where he must beat the competition without divulging his secret: he cannot use magic.

Mash manages to destroy the statue of the deity and defeat Carpaccio, the wielder of one of the Master Canes. Now, Mash must defeat his next opponent, Margarette Macaron, a sound magic caster and this year's frontrunner to win Divine Visionary. Before the battle commences, however, Divine Visionary Kaldo Gehenna, the head of magic talent administration, insists on assessing Mash's worthiness to become a Divine Visionary by challenging him to a game of you look, you lose.
