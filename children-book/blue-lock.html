Blue lock 12

Author
Muneyuki Kaneshiro, Yusuke Nomura
Publisher
Kim Đồng
Publishing date
2023

Summary

After suffering a devastating loss at the 2018 World Cup, Japan's team struggles to recover. But what is absent? A true Ace Striker who can lead them to victory. The Japan Football Union is hell-bent on creating a striker who hungers for goals and thirsts for victory, and who can be the deciding factor in turning a losing match around...and to do so, they have gathered 300 of Japan's most talented and promising junior players. Who will emerge as the team's leader, and will they be able to out-muscle and out-ego their opponents?

35 strikers made it through the second selection, and if they want to continue with Blue Lock, they must risk everything against the Japanese U-20 team! Is Isagi capable of proving himself against the top six strikers as the "Blue Lock Eleven" trials commence?
